## Packages
framer-motion | Essential for the playful animations and transitions between voting/results states
canvas-confetti | For the "Radical" and "Holy Cow" celebration effects
@types/canvas-confetti | Types for confetti
lucide-react | Icons for UI elements

## Notes
The app relies on the browser's localStorage to persist the 'voted' state for the day to prevent re-voting on reload.
Images will be served via Unsplash URLs in the backend or frontend if not provided by the API.
